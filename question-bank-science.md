# Question bank — science expansion (50 more)

Five "based" categories to add to `Domains`. Same bar as the original eighty: a real "huh" underneath, findable from one page, never spoiled. These also act as the **style anchors** for each new domain — feed a few from the relevant set to the generator so its first inventions for that category clear the bar instead of stumbling.

---

## Medicine & health
- Why does a cut on your skin heal but a cut nerve often doesn't?
- Why does a scab itch as it heals?
- Why do we run a fever when we're sick instead of just fighting the bug quietly?
- Why can't you remember being a baby?
- Why does a paper cut hurt more than a much bigger cut?
- Why does your voice sound wrong to you on a recording?
- Why do bruises cycle through all those colors as they fade?
- Why does looking at the horizon help with motion sickness?
- Why does stress give you an actual stomachache?
- Do we really still not fully understand how general anesthesia switches off consciousness?

## Biology & the living world
- Why don't woodpeckers give themselves concussions?
- Why do cats purr — and is it really only when they're happy?
- Why do we still have an appendix if we can live fine without it?
- Why can a starfish regrow a lost arm but we can't regrow a finger?
- How do migrating birds know which way to go?
- Why do mosquitoes bite some people far more than others?
- Why do trees stop getting taller at a certain point?
- Why do some animals' eyes glow in the dark when ours don't?
- Why do flowers smell — and who is the smell actually aimed at?
- Why does a dog tilt its head when you talk to it?

## Earth & geology
- Why are there seashell fossils on top of mountains?
- Where does beach sand actually come from?
- How do we know what's at the center of the Earth if nobody's been there?
- Why is the ocean salty when the rivers feeding it aren't?
- Why does the ground get hotter the deeper you dig?
- Why do diamonds and coal come from the same element but look nothing alike?
- Why are mountains still rising — what's pushing them up?
- Why do earthquakes hit some regions constantly and others almost never?
- Why does one volcano explode while another just oozes slowly?
- Why do some rocks form in neat stripes or layers?

## Math & numbers
- Why does anything raised to the power of zero equal one?
- Why can't you divide by zero?
- Why does multiplying two negative numbers make a positive?
- Why is a full circle 360 degrees specifically?
- Why does 0.999... repeating actually equal exactly 1?
- Why are there infinitely many prime numbers — and why does your bank care?
- Why can you only fold a piece of paper about seven times?
- Why do bees build their honeycomb in hexagons rather than squares or circles?
- Why does a shuffled deck almost certainly land in an order that has never existed before?
- Why did it take humans so long to invent the number zero?

## Space & sky
- Why doesn't the Moon just fall down onto us?
- Why is space pitch black when the Sun is blazing right there?
- Why does the same face of the Moon always point at Earth?
- Why doesn't the Sun burn out the way a fire does?
- Why does the Space Station stay up instead of falling back to Earth?
- Why is there no sound in space?
- Why can you see stars that might not even exist anymore?
- Do astronauts float because there's no gravity up there — or is that a myth?
- Why are sunsets red on Earth but blue on Mars?
- Why do the constellations always appear in the same shapes?

## Adding a category later — the routine
1. Add the domain name as a row in `Domains`.
2. Paste 8–10 hand-picked starters for it into `Queue` (a set like one above).
3. The generator picks the domain up in rotation and extends it, using those starters as the style anchor and the recent-questions list to avoid repeats.

Categories and questions both grow forever this way — the only thing you ever hand-tune is the first handful per new domain, and only to set the bar.
